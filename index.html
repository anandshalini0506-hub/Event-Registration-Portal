Hackathon Rules & Guidelines

  • All participants must register before the event begins
  • Teams can consist of 2-4 members. Solo participants are also welcome
  • You have exactly 24 hours to complete your project from the official start time
  • All code must be original and written during the hackathon period
  • Teams must submit their project through the official submission portal before the deadline
  • Violation of code of conduct may result in disqualification
  • Winners will be selected based on innovation, functionality, and presentation
